Research


My research is in commutative algebra and algebraic combinatorics.
    On the commutative algebra side, I am interested in questions about depth, multiplicities and measures of singularity, certain interesting types of categories and notions of radius and dimension of categories, and Cohen-Macaulay type.
    In algebraic combinatorics, I am interested in Stanley-Reisner theory and understanding certain homological conditions, such as bounds on depth or Serre conditions, from the simplicial complex perspective.
